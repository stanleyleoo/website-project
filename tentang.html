Tentang HMFH

We are an organization for the student of Information System. We are based in Medan, to be exact at Pelita Harapan University Medan. This organization was formed at February 6, 2016. We are lead by our coordinator who named Okky Putra Barus. We consist of 20 members who all are the year of 2015 student. There are some Divisions in our organization to help ease each other's workload. Each Division was lead by a coordinator to supervise the work of the divison. The organization's establishment actually starts from a club which has almost the same goals, but in a more specific way. The organization has a broader scope so the club eventually was under the supervision of this organization. The member who joined the organization was almost the same.
